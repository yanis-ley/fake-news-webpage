Politics

Fake news is not uncommon in politics, where it is used for political agitation.
The credibility of politicians or a government is to be shaken by false allegations and invented scandals.

Such an event took place in Germany, where it was alleged that Annalena Baerbock, the chancellor of the Greens, said that widows would soon receive less money. It is said that the politician wants to cut funds in this area and invest the money for the integration of refugees instead. This is how her opponents want to stir up the mood against Baerbock.
